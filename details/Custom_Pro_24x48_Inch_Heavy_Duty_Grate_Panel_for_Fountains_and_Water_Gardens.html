Custom Pro 24x48 Inch Heavy Duty Grate Panel

This premium grate panel is built to last, offering a perfect blend of function and style for your water garden. Designed to support a variety of water features, it provides a secure, seamless cover for basins, reservoirs, and fountains. Constructed from high-quality HDPE, it resists corrosion and water damage, ensuring long-term use in outdoor environments.

With a generous size of 24\" x 48\" x 2.5\", the panel is versatile enough to accommodate different pond and water feature setups. Its ability to be cut with a saw makes it ideal for custom installations, allowing you to create access points for submersible pumps or adjust the panel to fit unique basin designs. Whether you're working on a pondless waterfall or a disappearing garden basin, this panel is both durable and discreet.

Key Features

  • Rustproof and waterproof construction for long-lasting durability
  • Supports up to 1,000 pounds with recommended cinder block installation
  • Generous dimensions for versatile use in fountains, basins, and water features
  • Customizable fit with easy cutting options
  • Perfect for hiding basins, reservoirs, and supporting garden decorations

Why Choose This Product?

The Custom Pro grate panel combines strength with an elegant appearance, making it an excellent choice for both residential and commercial water garden projects. Its robust design ensures stability and security for your pond or waterfall, while the black finish complements natural landscapes and outdoor decor seamlessly.

Optimized for Performance

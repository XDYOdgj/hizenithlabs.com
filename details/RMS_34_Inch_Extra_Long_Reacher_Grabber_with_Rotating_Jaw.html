RMS 34 Inch Extra Long Reacher Grabber

The RMS 34-inch reacher grabber is a versatile tool designed to make everyday tasks easier, especially when reaching or retrieving items in hard-to-access places. Whether you're lifting a small object from the floor or grabbing something off a high shelf, this tool provides the perfect combination of reach, grip, and control.
